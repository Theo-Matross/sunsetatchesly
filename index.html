subjective Personality

She got this tape playing in my head. She got me thinking of some kids. I don't know how they would look, how tall they'd be. For now they had no face but they were in my mind in this family and she was their mother. She would cook the best meals. She would make their beds, read bed time stories to them and sing them lullabies. She'd come to bed ; we'd hardly sleep. In the morning she'd be the first to wake. She'd make a sumptuous breakfast and I would take our children to school; four of them but she still looked like a godess. She'd go to work and send nasty texts. When I'd arrived from work she'd take my coat and ask how my day was. She'd look at me with her squint eyes which I loved. She'd start cooking supper as our children played. She'd scold the eldest one, the hard-headed one, for bullying his siblings. She'd be the perfect wife and mother.
